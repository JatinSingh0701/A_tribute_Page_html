Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose was a prominent Indian freedom fighter who played a key role in India's struggle for Independence against British rule. Born on 23 January 1897 , in Cuttack, Orissa, Bose was the ninth child of a wealthy lawyer.

Bose studied at the University of Calcutta and later went to England to pursue higher studies. However, he was expelled from the University of Calcutta for participating in a student protest and was subsequently arrested for his political activities.

Bose eventually formed the Indian National Army (INA) to fight against British rule in India. He died in a plane crash in 1945 under mysterious circumstances, but his legacy continues to inspire Indians to this day.
